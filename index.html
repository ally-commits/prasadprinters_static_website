About Us

Introduction

Prasad Printers had its humble beginning in 1972 at Kulshekar about 8kms away from Mangalore, the portal town at Karnataka. Sri Thomas Patrao, started this press with two treadle printing machines catering to transport and hotel Industry..

Sri Praveen Patrao, a post graduate, took over the helm of affairs in 2003 after the death of his father Sri Thomas Patrao, the founder of Prasad Printers. He ventured into new avenues, adapting to the changing times, of the new technology, 'Prasad' made substantial investment in all sectors, pre press, press and post press. Over the past four decades, we at 'Prasad Printers' nurtured the company to deliver the best to our clients by our steady focus and dedication. Today at 'Prasad' we have state of art printing machinery that offers precision and produce finest everlasting prints.

Go Green

As an environmental conscious firm, we are committed to the future with our environmental and recycling actions to reduce waste with our Go Green policy. Being sustainable involves a commitment to balance the economic, social and environmental impact of whatever we do.

We mainly use vegetable oil based ink.

We offer high end recycled papers for our clients who are environmental conscious..

Our paper waste is sent for recycling.

We adopt the latest state of art technology machinery which consumes minimal power.

We all live in a world with finite resources and it is vital that we act in a sustainable way. Our organization’s existing policies and activities are constantly audited and upgraded for its present environmental impact. This helps to determine where improvements can be made to our present infrastructure and production methods. With our Go Green policy, regular reviews are conducted at our organization as a whole so that we have support and understanding of everyone in working towards an effective Green Policy.

By using reusable, refillable or recyclable products, we consider the environmental factors involved in production, use and disposal of the products we use. While conforming to hygiene, health and safety, our work places take care of our worker’s need for an acceptable working environment and we implement greener practices wherever possible .
